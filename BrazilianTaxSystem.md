# An explanatory note on the Brazilian Tax System
You may notice the CSV has price after Tax
The Brazilian Tax system charges products below 50$ an 17-20% state tax (called ICMS), and,for products above 50$, they also slap on a 60% tax on top, with a 30$ discount.
Also, it's a paperwork nightmare if the store you are buying from is not participating in the Remessa Conforme program, as not only tax will be higher, but the value will be unknown and will probably jumpscare you, it also has the risk of being held up at the checkpoint. This is why it's best that, if you are ordering from foreign stores, like AliExpress, you pick AliExpress Choice, as it is part of the Remessa Conforme Program, which gives you an lower, known tax, which is paid upfront and allows the product to just slip thru.
